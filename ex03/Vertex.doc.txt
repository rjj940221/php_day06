Vertex

The Vertex class handles 3d homogeneous vector points in the form (x axis, y axis, z axis (depth) and w ( disturbing))
the class is called int the form `new Vertex(array('x',=> 3.5, 'y',=> 3.5, 'z' => 3.5, 'w' => 1.0, , 'color'=>$color))`
where $color is an instance of the Color class.
attributes x,y and z ar mandatory
attributes w and color are optional and defaulted to 1 and (255,255,255) respectively
